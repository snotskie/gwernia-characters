🎲 Checks

Roll 2 or lower: Fail.

Roll 3 or 4: If Easy or Apt, round up to 5. Otherwise, fail.

Roll 5: Minor Success (comes with a bust).

Roll 6 or 7: Some effects round up to 8. Otherwise, Minor Success.

Roll 8, 9, or 10: Medium Success.

Roll 11 to 19: Major Success (comes with a boon).

Roll 20+: Maximum Success (exceptional boon).

🪨 Difficulty

Hard: Roll twice, need to succeed both.

Complex: At least three rolls, with different Stats and/or characters per roll, fail at most once.

🤝 Helping

Once per roll you may Help another character by spending ①. They add half the value of that Blessing to the roll, rounded down, to a minimum of 1. They also gain an additional bust. You cannot Help if you are engaged in another action or if it would be unreasonable.

🔄 Attacks

Only Attacker rolls. If Defender has a relevant Apt, Check is one rank harder. If the Apt is Spec'd, it's another rank harder.

🪙 Gains

Within reason, you may gain things in the moment as though you already had it—such as items, info, allies, or rivals—, by performing Checks.

The less believable, the more difficult.

Failure could damage existing gains: drop an item, strain a relationship, or complicate the facts, for example.

⚔️ Vice & Virtue

Gain Blessings for completing important story milestones.

On your first Major Success each session, gain a Blessing. Each other player whose character witnessed or heard about that success also gains a Blessing.

📜 Schools
  • Elemental
    🍄 Herbs, seeds, spices, clays, boughs, oils, gems, awareness. 🔵 Make flower bloom, light a campfire. 🟢 Control wind, cure fatigue, throw sound. 🟠 Cure poison, throw fire, call rain, stealth. 🟣 Control and intensify the elements, control storms. ⚫ Summon and control elementals. ⚪ Force evolution.
  • Naming
    🍄 Inks, parchment, wax, silk, thread, staffs, oils, gems, precision. 🔵 Encrypt information, clean. 🟢 Understand magical language, repair. 🟠 Boost and dampen physical and mental capabilities. 🟣 Mind read, charm, suggest, transmute. ⚫ Teleport, divine fate, scry, speak with dragons. ⚪ Banish.
  • Star
    🍄 Salts, dusts, powders, tattoos, wands, oils, gems, movement. 🔵 Navigate, light paths. 🟢 Create illusion, send a cryptic message. 🟠 Adjust gravity or weight, create pure light. 🟣 Heal wounds, remove curses, enchant, shapeshift. ⚫ Adjust luck or fate, smite. ⚪ Time travel.
  • Necromancy
    🍄 Bones, blood, viscera, oils, gems, pacts. 🔵 Create acid, wither plants. 🟢 Roughen terrain, hear ethereal whispers. 🟠 Create darkness, infect, exhaust, speak with dead. 🟣 Drain energy, puppet undead, curse. ⚫ Summon undead, skinwalk, steal capabilities. ⚪ Cheat death.
  • Mechanics
    🍄 Tools, materials, workspace. 🔵 Create mundane item. 🟢 Create mundane item that helps overcome an obstacle, identify trap. 🟠 Create high-quality item that rounds 7 up to 8, pick lock, disable trap, identify magical device. 🟣 Create powered item that rounds 6 or 7 up to 8, disable magical device. ⚫ Create unique item that provides unique effects, change function of magical device. ⚪ Forge legendary artifact.
  • Alchemy
    🍄 Metals, powders, oils, salts, acids, bases, instruments, heat, formulae. 🔵 Identify substance. 🟢 Create explosive potions, identify magical properties. 🟠 Create potions that treat or cause mundane conditions, purify substance. 🟣 Create potions that treat or cause magical conditions, heighten magical properties, transmute substance. ⚫ Create homunculi. ⚪ Observe Truth.

Key: 🍄 Reagents. 🔵 Easy effects. 🟢 Normal effects. 🟠 Hard effects. 🟣 Complex effects. ⚫ Extra complex effects. ⚪ Quest-level effects.

Increase the difficulty if you are pushing the area, duration, or nature of the effect further than normal; if the effect requires a material cost you cannot pay; if you are untrained in the effect or it is outside your school of training; if the effect requires forbidden knowledge; or if the environment is not conducive to such effects.

Reagents are required to craft items and cast spells. You may expend high quality reagents and spend a Blessing to grant yourself Help on a related roll.
